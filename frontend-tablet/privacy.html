Privacy Policy

Protecting your data and privacy

1. What data we collect

We collect and process the following information to manage your loyalty account:

  • Full Name: To identify you in our system and personalise your rewards.
  • Email Address: To track your rewards and send you account updates or marketing (if opted in).
  • Visit History: The date, time, and spend amount of each visit.
  • Loyalty Tier: Your current status (Bronze, Silver, Gold).
  • Marketing Consent: Whether you have opted in to receive updates, including a secure timestamp.

2. Why we collect it (Legal Basis)

We process your data under the UK GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation):

  • Legitimate Interests: To operate the loyalty programme, calculate your rewards, and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: For sending marketing communications. You can withdraw your consent at any time via the "unsubscribe" link in any email.

4. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as you are an active member. Automatic Deletion: If you do not visit for 12 months, your account and all associated data are permanently deleted from our primary database.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You can also request a copy of your visit history or ask us to restrict the processing of your information.
