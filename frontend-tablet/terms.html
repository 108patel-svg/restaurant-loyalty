Terms & Conditions & Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Restaurant Loyalty Programme. These Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy govern your participation in our rewards scheme. By submitting the check-in form and participating in the programme, you agree to be bound by these terms.

2. What data we collect

We collect and process the following information when you check in:

  • Full name: To identify you in our system.
  • Email address: For account tracking and communication.
  • Spend amount: To calculate your loyalty rewards.
  • Visit history: The date and time of each visit.
  • Marketing consent: Whether you have opted in to receive updates, including the date and time of consent.
  • Loyalty tier: Your current status within the programme.

3. Why we collect it (legal basis)

We process your data under two lawful bases defined by the UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate Interests: To operate the loyalty programme, track your rewards, and provide you with the services you signed up for.
  • Consent: Specifically for sending marketing emails. Marketing consent is entirely optional and separate from your ability to participate in the loyalty programme.

4. How we use the data

Your data is used specifically for the following purposes:

  • To track and calculate your loyalty tier status automatically.
  • To display your reward progress on-screen during the check-in process.
  • To send automated tier-up notification emails (only if you have provided marketing consent).
  • To allow our staff to verify your visit history and apply discounts via the admin dashboard.

5. Who we share it with

We value your privacy. We only share data with essential service providers:

  • SendGrid: Our email delivery service (used only if you consent to marketing). You can view their policy at sendgrid.com.

We never sell your data to third parties, nor do we share it with advertisers.

7. How long we keep it

We retain your data for as long as you are an active member of the loyalty programme. If you do not visit for a period of 12 months, your data will be automatically deleted. You may also request earlier deletion at any time.

7. Loyalty tracking disclosure

Please be aware that every time you scan the QR code and submit the check-in form, your visit is recorded. Your cumulative spend over a rolling 90-day window is calculated automatically to determine your tier. This tracking is used solely for the purpose of rewards and you are informed of your status on every visit.

8. Customer rights (UK GDPR)

Under the UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be informed: Clear information about how we use your data (provided here).
  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to erasure: The "right to be forgotten"—you can ask us to delete your data.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask us to temporarily stop using your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to our use of your data for certain purposes.
  • Rights related to automated decision making: You have rights regarding decisions made without human intervention (like your tier calculation).

9. How to exercise your rights

To exercise any of your rights, please email us at loyalty@yourrestaurant.com with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

10. Cookies

This application uses localStorage (browser-based storage) to maintain system settings and session data in demo mode. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will notify you via email if you have consented to marketing communications.

12. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at loyalty@yourrestaurant.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
