1. Eligibility

Participation in the the restaurant loyalty programme is open to individuals aged 18 and over. By checking in, you agree to these terms.

2. Earning Rewards

Rewards are earned based on your total spend and visit frequency. The restaurant reserves the right to adjust reward thresholds or discount percentages at any time.

3. Loyalty Tiers

Your "Tier" (Bronze, Silver, Gold) is determined by your total spend over a rolling 90-day window. If your spend drops below the threshold during this window, your tier status may be adjusted accordingly.

4. Use of Discounts

Loyalty discounts apply to the total bill and cannot be combined with other offers, vouchers, or set menus unless explicitly stated by the restaurant manager.

5. Account Termination

We reserve the right to terminate any loyalty account if we suspect fraudulent activity or abuse of the rewards system. As per our data policy, inactive accounts (no visits for 12 months) are automatically closed.

6. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Your continued use of the loyalty programme constitutes acceptance of any changes.
