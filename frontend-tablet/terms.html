Loyalty Programme

Terms & Conditions & Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the loyalty programme of the restaurant. By joining, you agree to these terms. This loyalty programme is open to customers aged 18 and over. If you are under 18, please do not submit your details.

2. What data we collect

We collect and process the following information when you check in:

  • Full name: To identify you in our system.
  • Email address: For account tracking and communication.
  • Phone number: Optional, for secondary identification.
  • Marketing consent: Whether you have opted in to receive updates, including the date and time of consent.
  • Visit history: The date, time, and spend of each visit.
  • Loyalty tier: Your current status within the programme.

3. Why we collect it (legal basis)

We process your data under two lawful bases defined by the UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate Interests: To operate the loyalty programme and track your rewards.
  • Consent: Specifically for sending marketing emails. Marketing consent is entirely optional and can be withdrawn at any time.

5. How long we keep it

We retain your data for as long as you are an active member. If you do not visit for 12 months, your data is automatically deleted. You can also request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can also withdraw your marketing consent via the unsubscribe link in any email.
