How Rewards Work

1. Loyalty Tiers

The system tracks a customer's total spend over the Tracking Window (usually 90 days). As they spend more, they automatically move from Bronze to Silver to Gold. They receive their tier's discount on every visit.

2. Retention Bonus

If a customer returns within the Retention Period (e.g., 14 days), they receive a higher discount. The system always gives them the best possible discount between their Tier and this bonus.

3. Frequency Reward

If a customer visits X times within Y days, they get a special frequency discount. This encourages them to visit more often in a short period.

4. Visit Milestones

This is a one-time (or repeating) gift. When their total lifetime visit count hits your target (e.g., 10th visit), the tablet will show a "Milestone Reached" message with a specific gift like "Free Dessert".

💡 Pro Tip

All these rules are dynamic. If you change a threshold in the Settings tab, the app instantly recalculates everyone's status!
