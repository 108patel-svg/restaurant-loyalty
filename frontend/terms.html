Terms and Conditions & Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our restaurant loyalty programme. This document sets out the Terms and Conditions and our Privacy Policy regarding how we handle your personal data. By submitting the check-in form and participating in the loyalty programme, you agree to these terms.

2. What data we collect

To operate our loyalty programme, we explicitly collect and store the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • The amount spent on each of your visits
  • The date and time of each visit
  • Whether you have consented to receive marketing emails, and the date of that consent
  • Your current loyalty tier status

3. Why we collect it (Legal basis)

We process your data under the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate Interests: We need to process your visit and spend data to operate the loyalty programme, calculate your rewards, and provide you with discounts.
  • Consent: We only send you promotional and marketing emails if you have explicitly ticked the consent box. You can participate fully in the loyalty programme without consenting to marketing communications.

4. How we use the data

Your personal data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To track your loyalty tier status and calculate the rewards you are entitled to.
  • To display your progress and current tier on screen when you check in at the restaurant.
  • To send you tier-up notification emails and monthly updates (only if you have consented to marketing).
  • To allow our staff to view customer visit history securely via an internal administrative dashboard.

5. Who we share it with

We respect your privacy. We never sell your data to third parties, and we do not share your data with advertisers.

If you have consented to receive marketing emails, your name and email address will be shared securely with SendGrid, our email delivery service provider, purely for the purpose of sending those emails on our behalf.

6. How long we keep it

Your customer data is retained for as long as you are an active member of the loyalty programme. If you do not visit the restaurant for a continuous period of 12 months after your last visit, your data will be automatically deleted from our systems. You may also request earlier deletion at any time.

7. Loyalty tracking disclosure

Please be aware of how the tracking works:

  • Every time you visit, scan the QR code, and submit the check-in form, that visit is recorded.
  • Your cumulative spend over a rolling 90-day window is calculated automatically to determine your tier.
  • This tracking is used solely to provide you with loyalty rewards.
  • You are informed of your current tracking status and progress on every visit confirmation screen.

8. Your rights (UK GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to be informed: You have the right to know how your data is used (which this policy fulfils).
  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: Also known as the "right to be forgotten", you can ask us to delete your data from our systems.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask us to pause processing your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You can object to us processing your data for direct marketing (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Rights related to automated decision making: You have rights regarding decisions made without human involvement. Our tier calculations are automated, but they only provide benefits and do not produce legal or significantly adverse effects.

9. How to exercise your rights

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please email us at [INSERT RESTAURANT EMAIL] with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

10. Cookies

Our application uses your browser's local storage to remember your details temporarily during the check-in process. We do not use tracking cookies. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via email (provided you have consented to marketing communications).

12. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at [INSERT RESTAURANT EMAIL].

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
